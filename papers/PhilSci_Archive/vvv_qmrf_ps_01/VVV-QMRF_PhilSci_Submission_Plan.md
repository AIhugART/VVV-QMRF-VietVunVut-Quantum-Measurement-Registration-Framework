## RCA Principle for This Plan

This plan is not only a submission workflow. It is a publication-safety workflow.

The paper may proceed to PhilSci Archive only after every major claim declares:

1. source authority,
2. claim class,
3. registration-layer boundary,
4. formula status,
5. verification rule,
6. known limitation.

Safe framing:

> VVV-QMRF proposes a K-side registration-layer framework for questions left under-specified by the P1–P4 formalism of Standard Quantum Mechanics. It does not replace Standard Quantum Mechanics, does not modify the Born rule, does not claim consciousness causes collapse, and does not treat Buddhist Epistemology as physical theory.

Unsafe framing to avoid:

> Buddhist Epistemology solves Quantum Measurement.  
> Quantum Mechanics is defective.  
> E1 alone resolves the measurement problem.  
> Self-awareness collapses the wavefunction.

- “remains compatible with decoherence and interpretation-specific accounts.”

#### Section 4: E6 — Registering-System-as-Process as Domain Prerequisite

This section must come before E1.

Required claim:

> E6 defines the registration-layer domain within which measurement-registration acts are typed. E1 may apply `σ` only to `Mᵢ ∈ R` after E6 supplies `R`.

Required formal sketch:

```text
R = ⟨M₁, M₂, ..., Mₙ; Π̂_causal⟩
Mᵢ ∈ R
```

Required boundary:

- E6 is not a physical observer theory.
- E6 is not a claim about consciousness.
- E6 is not an additional physical system inside Hilbert space.
- E6 does not remove the Heisenberg cut physically; it reframes the K-side registering role.

#### Section 5: E1 — Self-Certifying Registration as Typed Application

Required claim:

> Given an E6-supplied process domain `R`, a typed K-side measurement-registration act `Mᵢ ∈ R` carries an occurrence-certification marker `σ(Mᵢ)` without requiring a second K-side meta-registration act `M′`.

Required formal sketch:

```text
Given E6 domain: R = ⟨M₁, M₂, ..., Mₙ; Π̂_causal⟩
For Mᵢ ∈ R:
  σ(Mᵢ) ∈ {0,1}
  σ(Mᵢ) = 1 iff Mᵢ has occurred as a K-side registration event
  σ(Mᵢ) is determined by Mᵢ itself, not by M′ ≠ Mᵢ
```

Required boundary:

- E1 stops only the K-side meta-registration regress.
- E1 does not prevent a larger ρ-side physical description of apparatus or environment.
- E1 does not claim consciousness or introspection.
- E1 does not modify probability amplitudes, the Born rule, or Hamiltonian dynamics.

#### Section 6: E2 — Registration Self-Completion as Downstream Closure

Required claim:

> E2 develops the K-side act-result closure that becomes meaningful only after E6 supplies the typed registration domain and E1 supplies occurrence certification.

Required boundary:

- E2 is not a physical identity claim inside Hilbert space.
- E2 does not say all quantum outcomes physically collapse because of K-side closure.
- E2 is a registration-layer closure claim.

#### Section 7: Extended Architecture — E3–E16, S1–S2, Categories 01–15

This section must be a summary unless the paper adds Appendix B.

Required handling:
