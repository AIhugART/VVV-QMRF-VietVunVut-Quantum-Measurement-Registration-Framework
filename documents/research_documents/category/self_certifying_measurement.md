## 3. Formal Structure / Cấu trúc Hình thức

**English:**
In standard QM, if Apparatus A measures System S, A becomes entangled with S. To know A's state, Apparatus B must measure A, leading to an infinite regress. Under this new category:
1. **The Reflexive Act:** The measurement interaction Hamiltonian is modified to include a self-referential trace component.
2. **Registration Certification:** The operator $\hat{R}_{svasa}$ acts such that the macroscopic state transition of Apparatus A is self-luminous (svaprakāśa). The event $E_A$ is mathematically complete and verified at the exact moment it occurs.
3. **Regress Termination:** The probability amplitude vector structurally "seals" itself. Asking "Who measured Apparatus A?" becomes a mathematically ill-posed question, just as asking "Who lights up the Sun?" is physically meaningless.
