## Framework lens key

- A1 Dependent Arising: relational conditioning, causal dependence, system-meter coupling, process, correlation, trajectory.
- A2 Emptiness: absence of intrinsic properties, critique of substance, self, realism, universals, or pre-existing determinate properties.
- A3 Consciousness-Only: cognition, perception, measurement, state assignment, manifestation through epistemic relation, state update.
- A4 Two Truths: conventional vs ultimate description, classical vs quantum tiering, conceptual construction, language, interpretation, Heisenberg cut.

## RCA rules

- Each row is 1:1 from one BE system element to one QM concept.
- Repeated QM targets allowed (263 nodes + 226 edges map to 105 QM concepts).
- Cross-domain links are structural analogies only, not doctrinal, physical, or ontological equivalences.
- `Strong` = close formal or functional analogy.
- `Medium` = clear structural analogy without formal equivalence.
- `Weak` = nearest available 1:1 anchor with limited analogy.
- BIAN labels (BIAN-1 through BIAN-20) identify structural BE concepts absent from QM formalism per canonical BIAN Index.
