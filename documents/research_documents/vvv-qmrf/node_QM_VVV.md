## 1. RCA Decision Summary

`SYSTEM_Quantum_Measurement/system_qm_full.md` already contains the physical basis for several concepts: `N_QM_00033` No-Result Measurement, `N_QM_00032` Partial Wavefunction Collapse, `N_QM_00018` Projection Operator, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, `N_QM_00027` Information-Disturbance Trade-off, and `N_QM_00005` Superposition.

However, `category/vvv_qmrf_category_01_e11_purely_contrastive_evidence.md` introduces concepts that are not present as canonical QM nodes. The new items are VVV-QMRF registration, inferential, or formal-category extensions built on top of existing QM nodes. They are therefore assigned `N_QM_VVV_XXXXX` codes, not canonical `N_QM_XXXXX` codes.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_04_e07_dual_phase_registration_certification.md` also reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00019` Measurement, `N_QM_00021` System-Meter Coupling, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, `N_QM_00025` Density Matrix, `N_QM_00095` Decoherence & Environment as Measurement, `N_QM_00103` Quantum Feedback and Control, and `N_QM_00105` Continuous Quantum Error Correction. Its new content is not a new physical measurement substrate; it is a VVV-QMRF formalization of measurement-validity location, external certification, and certified registration status. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00011` and higher.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_13_e14_validated_absence_registration.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00033` No-Result Measurement, `N_QM_00018` Projection Operator, and `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, and overlaps with existing VVV null-evidence nodes such as `N_QM_VVV_00001`, `N_QM_VVV_00003`, `N_QM_VVV_00004`, and `N_QM_VVV_00005`. Its new content is not the physical null result or the projection formula by itself; it is the BIAN-9 registration category that treats absence/non-perception as valid absence registration under explicit validity conditions. This addition is assigned `N_QM_VVV_00020`; `Π̂_absent` is folded into `N_QM_VVV_00003` rather than separately re-coded.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_08_e03_registration_lock_operation.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00019` Measurement, `N_QM_00020` von Neumann Measurement Model, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, and `N_QM_00094` Heisenberg Cut. Its new content is not the physical pointer trace, collapse, or measurement chain by itself; it is a VVV-QMRF registration layer that converts a macroscopic measurement record into internal representation, registration lock, and registered status. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00021` and higher.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_14_e15_intrinsic_relational_binding.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00045` Tensor Product Space, `N_QM_00046` Product State, `N_QM_00047` Entanglement, `N_QM_00051` Composite Observables, `N_QM_00090` Bell's Inequality & Bell Correlations, and `N_QM_00091` Hidden Variable Theories, while using Dharmakīrti's causal/identity contrast classes only as source-side contrasts. Its new content is not the physical fact of entanglement or Bell violation by itself; it is a VVV-QMRF relation category that treats Bell-nonlocal entanglement as registration-layer non-separability beyond causal models and Tādātmya-style logical genus-species identity. This addition is assigned `N_QM_VVV_00025`; the `\mathcal{E}_{svabh}` symbol is folded into that root category rather than separately coded.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_02_e02_registration_self_completion_matrix.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00019` Measurement, `N_QM_00021` System-Meter Coupling, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, and `N_QM_00016` Born Rule. Its new content is a VVV-QMRF act-result registration identity: a valid detector response and its K-side registered result form one closure event. This addition is assigned `N_QM_VVV_00027` and `N_QM_VVV_00028`; ordinary Born-rule probability and physical collapse remain canonical QM support.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_03_e08_retroactive_registration_override.md` reuses canonical QM material such as measurement, state update, decoherence, reversal, feedback, and error correction. Its new content is not a time-reversal of physical history; it is a K-side registration-validity override that can demote a prior detector response to registration-error status. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00029` through `N_QM_VVV_00032`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_05_e01_self_certifying_registration_operator.md` reuses the von Neumann chain and Heisenberg Cut problem but adds a VVV-QMRF regress-stopping category. Its new content is the proposed self-certifying registration operator and primary closure status, not a new physical collapse mechanism. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00033` through `N_QM_VVV_00035`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_06_e09_null_registering_system_event.md` reuses system-meter coupling, no-result measurement, unselective measurement, and decoherence, but its new content is the K-side state where physical interaction occurs without valid registration encoding. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00036` through `N_QM_VVV_00038`; ordinary failed detection and detector efficiency remain engineering or canonical support unless they carry registration-layer classification.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_07_e06_registering_system_as_process_framework.md` reuses quantum trajectory, continuous measurement, quantum jumps, and environment-as-measurement as support. Its new content is a VVV-QMRF registering-side architecture: no persistent classical registering entity is required; only causally linked momentary registering events. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00039` through `N_QM_VVV_00041`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_09_e10_tripartite_registration_validity_matrix.md` reuses measurement, system-meter coupling, detector fidelity, and noise concepts, but adds a three-condition registration-validity gate inspired by Trairūpya. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00042` and `N_QM_VVV_00043`; individual Trairūpya conditions are kept inside the criteria-set node rather than split into three separate nodes.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_10_e04_pre_symbolic_stratum.md` reuses physical interaction, von Neumann measurement, PVM, generalized measurement, and weak measurement. Its new content is not mystical consciousness or a new physical interaction; it is the missing registration layer between physical event and symbolic/eigenvalue result. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00044` through `N_QM_VVV_00047`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_11_e12_limit_faculty_registration.md` reuses weak measurement, weak value, generalized eigenvalues, quantum-limited sensing, SQL, and QND measurement. Its new content is the registration-validity category for non-ordinary but valid measurement faculties beyond ordinary projective eigenvalue-only registration. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00048` through `N_QM_VVV_00050`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_12_e13_temporal_discontinuity_doctrine.md` reuses continuous evolution, Schrödinger dynamics, wavefunction collapse, continuous quantum jumps, and jump operators. Its new content is a registration-layer treatment of quantum jumps as bounded kṣaṇa registration events. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00051` through `N_QM_VVV_00053`.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_15_e16_pre_measurement_registration_indeterminacy.md` reuses superposition, density matrices, Bayesian update, hidden-variable debate, and classical-vs-quantum indeterminacy. Its new content is a K-side structured registration-doubt state before measurement: not ignorance, not certainty, and not a hidden variable. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00054` and `N_QM_VVV_00055`.
