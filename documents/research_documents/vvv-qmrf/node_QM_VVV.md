## 1. RCA Decision Summary

`SYSTEM_Quantum_Measurement/system_qm_full.md` already contains the physical basis for several concepts: `N_QM_00033` No-Result Measurement, `N_QM_00032` Partial Wavefunction Collapse, `N_QM_00018` Projection Operator, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, `N_QM_00027` Information-Disturbance Trade-off, and `N_QM_00005` Superposition.

However, `category/vvv_qmrf_category_01_e11_purely_contrastive_evidence.md` introduces concepts that are not present as canonical QM nodes. The new items are VVV-QMRF registration, inferential, or formal-category extensions built on top of existing QM nodes. They are therefore assigned `N_QM_VVV_XXXXX` codes, not canonical `N_QM_XXXXX` codes.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_04_e07_dual_phase_registration_certification.md` also reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00019` Measurement, `N_QM_00021` System-Meter Coupling, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, `N_QM_00025` Density Matrix, `N_QM_00095` Decoherence & Environment as Measurement, `N_QM_00103` Quantum Feedback and Control, and `N_QM_00105` Continuous Quantum Error Correction. Its new content is not a new physical measurement substrate; it is a VVV-QMRF formalization of measurement-validity location, external certification, and certified registration status. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00011` and higher.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_13_e14_validated_absence_registration.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00033` No-Result Measurement, `N_QM_00018` Projection Operator, and `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, and overlaps with existing VVV null-evidence nodes such as `N_QM_VVV_00001`, `N_QM_VVV_00003`, `N_QM_VVV_00004`, and `N_QM_VVV_00005`. Its new content is not the physical null result or the projection formula by itself; it is the BIAN-9 registration category that treats absence/non-perception as valid absence registration under explicit validity conditions. This addition is assigned `N_QM_VVV_00020`; `Π̂_absent` is folded into `N_QM_VVV_00003` rather than separately re-coded.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_08_e03_registration_lock_operation.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00019` Measurement, `N_QM_00020` von Neumann Measurement Model, `N_QM_00022` Post-Measurement State Update, and `N_QM_00094` Heisenberg Cut. Its new content is not the physical pointer trace, collapse, or measurement chain by itself; it is a VVV-QMRF registration layer that converts a macroscopic measurement record into internal representation, registration lock, and registered status. These additions are assigned `N_QM_VVV_00021` and higher.

`category/vvv_qmrf_category_14_e15_intrinsic_relational_binding.md` reuses canonical QM material such as `N_QM_00045` Tensor Product Space, `N_QM_00046` Product State, `N_QM_00047` Entanglement, `N_QM_00051` Composite Observables, `N_QM_00090` Bell's Inequality & Bell Correlations, `N_QM_00091` Hidden Variable Theories, and `N_QM_00021` System-Meter Coupling. Its new content is not the physical fact of entanglement or Bell violation by itself; it is a VVV-QMRF relation category that treats Bell-nonlocal entanglement as registration-layer non-separability beyond causal models and Tādātmya-style logical genus-species identity. This addition is assigned `N_QM_VVV_00025`; the `E_svabh` symbol is folded into that root category rather than separately coded.
