# Buddhist Epistemology to Quantum Measurement — 1:1 RCA Mapping

## Scope

This is a line-by-line RCA mapping from the Buddhist Epistemology concept table to the Quantum Measurement concept table.

- Ground system: Buddhist Epistemology.
- Target system: Quantum Measurement.
- Mapping rule: each Buddhist Epistemology row maps to exactly one Quantum Measurement row.
- Repeated Quantum Measurement targets are allowed because the Buddhist table has 232 rows and the Quantum Measurement table has 105 rows.
- Cross-domain links are structural analogies only, not claims of doctrinal, physical, or historical equivalence.
- No external concepts are introduced outside the two source tables.

## RCA labels

- Strong: close formal or functional analogy.
- Medium: clear structural analogy, but not formal equivalence.
- Weak: nearest available 1:1 anchor; analogy is limited.
- BIAN: Buddhist Insight with No direct Quantum Measurement analogue; forced 1:1 anchor is supplied only for table completeness.
