Author: VietVunVut (Viet - Nguyen Xuan); GitHub: https://github.com/AIhugART/; Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/xuanviet

## Purpose

This document provides a complete index of 20 BIAN (Buddhist-Interpretive Absent Node) labels identifying structural concepts in Buddhist Epistemology (BE) that have no direct counterpart in Quantum Measurement (QM) formalism. Each entry includes full node-level mapping columns and all related edges extracted from the 1:1 RCA mapping.

## Source

- Mapping file: `documents/research_documents/mapping/system_be_qm_framework_1to1_RCA_mapping.md`
- BE system: 263 nodes, 226 edges (`SYSTEM_Buddhist_Epistemology/system_be_full.md`)
- QM target: 105 concepts (`documents/published_documents/QM_Measurement_Unified_Concept_Table.md`)

## Conventions

- BIAN labels range from BIAN-1 to BIAN-20.
- Node codes follow the format `N_BE_XXXXX`; edge codes follow `ED_BE_XXXXX`.
- Framework lenses: A1 = Dependent Arising, A2 = Emptiness, A3 = Consciousness-Only, A4 = Two Truths.
- RCA levels: Strong, Medium, Weak.
- Dash (—) indicates no separate node exists in the 263-node BE system.
